The Legendary Leafs

The Legendary Leafs, this franchise has been the richest franchise for a number of years and has many fans worldwide. The Leafs being founded in November 22, 1917 is one of the oldest teams in the NHL and have the second most Stanley cups, second only to the Montreal Canadiens. The Maple Leafs biggest rivals are the Motreal Canadiens, Ottawa Senators and most recently the Boston Bruins. This year the leafs have a young but explosive roster with power houses on offense, wiith new accusations like the captain John Tavares, defensemen Jake Muzzin the leafs now have a chance to win their first stanley cup since 1967. Hockeys Golden Age

The NHL is a billion dollar industry with some of the most talented athletes in the world. With teams in Big markets like Toronto and Chicago, as well as small markets like Florida proves the NHL's is rapidly expanding. The NHL is Canadas biggest sports with a large number of its players hailing from Canada, including some of its greatest players Sidney Crosby, Drew Doughty, Nathan Mackinnon and many more. The NHL has a daunting 82 game schedule followed by a competitive playoffs in order to win "Lord Stanleys Mug", The Stanley Cup. With all this talent this is certainly a golden age for the NHL.
